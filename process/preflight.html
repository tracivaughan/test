The Pre-flight Readiness Check brings together the key members of a project team to outline how they plan to accomplish a product goal or work request. The group discusses and agrees on subjects including:

  • What do we know so far about the project/request?
  • What additional information will we need to gather to build a successful product?
  • What methodology or process will we follow?
  • What roles and functions are needed for this project, and how are they assigned?
  • How will project management tasks be divided among the team?
  • Where and how are team decisions documented?

Deliverables and outcomes

  • Completed Pre-flight Readiness Check
  • Scheduled date for project kickoff meeting
